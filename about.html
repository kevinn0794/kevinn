About Us

School Overview

GS Gahurire-TSS is a government-aided technical secondary school located in Gahurire Sector, Rwanda. The school combines general education with technical and vocational training, shaping students into knowledgeable and skilled individuals ready for the workforce and higher education.

History

Founded in [insert year], GS Gahurire-TSS has evolved from a basic secondary school to an institution that provides high-quality technical training. It plays a key role in the educational development of the local community.
