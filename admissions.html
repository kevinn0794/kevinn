Admissions

Admission Overview

At GS Gahurire-TSS, we welcome motivated students who are eager to pursue both general and technical education. Our admissions process is designed to be clear, fair, and supportive of students from diverse backgrounds.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form (available online or at the school).
  • Academic transcripts from the most recent school attended.
  • Copy of national ID (for students above 16) or birth certificate.
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs.
  • Recommendation letter (for technical program applicants).
  • Proof of previous technical studies (for direct entry into advanced levels).

Application Process

The application process consists of the following steps:

  1. Obtain and fill out the application form.
  2. Submit the form with required documents to the school office or online portal.
  3. For technical programs, attend an interview or entry test if required.
  4. Receive admission decision via email, phone, or posted notice.
  5. Pay the registration and school fees to confirm your place.

Important Dates

  • Applications Open: June 1
  • Application Deadline: August 15
  • Entrance Exams (if applicable): September 5
  • Academic Year Begins: Mid-September

Why Choose GS Gahurire-TSS?

  • Combination of academic and practical technical education
  • Qualified teaching staff and well-equipped workshops
  • Strong reputation and support for graduates entering careers or universities
  • Safe, inclusive, and student-centered environment
