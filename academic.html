Academic Programs

GS Gahurire-TSS offers a broad and dynamic curriculum designed to meet the needs of a diverse student body. We combine traditional academic subjects with technical and vocational education to ensure students are well-equipped for both higher education and employment.

Subjects and Departments

  • Mathematics and Sciences: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics for analytical and scientific thinking.
  • Languages: English, French, and Kinyarwanda to develop communication and literacy skills.
  • Humanities: History, Geography, and Entrepreneurship to broaden world understanding and business acumen.
  • ICT: Digital literacy, programming, and computer systems.

Technical & Vocational Education

As a Technical Secondary School, we place strong emphasis on practical, hands-on training in specialized fields. Students can choose from:

  • Construction Technology: Includes masonry, drawing, and civil engineering principles.
  • Electricity: Covers domestic and industrial installation, electronics, and renewable energy basics.
  • ICT (Information and Communication Technology): Networking, system maintenance, and software applications.

Curriculum Overview

The curriculum is guided by Rwanda's national education framework. It blends general education with technical training to develop both knowledge and employable skills. Continuous assessment, practical evaluations, and national examinations ensure academic rigor and student preparedness.

Assessment Methods

  • Termly exams and projects
  • National examinations by REB and RTB
  • Practical technical assessments
  • Continuous assessment tests (CATs)

Career Pathways

Upon graduation, students are well-prepared to pursue:

  • Higher education at universities or polytechnics
  • Vocational training or apprenticeships
  • Direct employment in technical fields like construction, electrical installation, or ICT support
